A09:2025 Security Logging & Alerting Failures

Insufficient logging prevents detection of breaches and blocks forensic analysis.

Challenges
  1. Password in logs: Login at /a09/login-bad (fail and succeed). Check /a09/view-logs — your password is in the log!
  2. Silent failure: Compare logs from /a09/login-bad vs /a09/login-good. Notice auth failures are invisible in the bad logger.
  3. Large transfer: Submit a $9,999 transfer at /a09/transfer-bad — no alert. Then do the same at /a09/transfer-good — anomaly detected.
  4. Full CC in logs: Submit a transfer with a card number at /a09/transfer-bad. Find the full card number in the logs.
